Aleksey Gurtovoy

Aleksey Gurtovoy is an entrepreneur, engineer and product builder. He is the original author of the Boost Metaprogramming Library and a co-author, with David Abrahams, of C++ Template Metaprogramming.

He has been working with C++ since 1993, and holds a M.S. degree in computer science from Krasnoyarsk State Technical University, Russia.

You can contact him by email at agurtovoy@acm.org and follow him on Twitter at @agurtovoy.
