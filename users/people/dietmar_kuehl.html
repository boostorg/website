Dietmar Kuehl

Dietmar Kuehl (the "ue" is actually a "u-umlaut", ie. one of those funny German characters which has two dots above it) was fork(2)ed in early 1968.

He visited school more or less successfully from 1973 to 1987. In 1987 he started to study at the Technical University of Berlin. He finished his studies in 1997 with a Diplom (roughly the German equivalent of a masters) in Mathematics. His thesis was "Design Pattern for the Implementation of Graph Algorithms"; strike the "Design Pattern" and put in "Generic" somehow to get an idea of the main topic.

Since 1997 he has worked as consultant for a small company called Claas Solutions (the "aa" is no typo), mainly working for major German banks. Since late 1995 Dietmar Kuehl has been one of the moderators of the newsgroup comp.lang.c++.moderated. He is active on the C++ Standards committee.
